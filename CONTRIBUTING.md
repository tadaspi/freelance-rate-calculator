# Contributing to Freelance Rate Calculator

Thanks for your interest in contributing to this freelance hourly rate calculator!

## How to Use the Calculator

**Live Calculator:** [https://hourly-rate-calculator.tp-business.workers.dev](https://hourly-rate-calculator.tp-business.workers.dev)

The calculator helps freelancers determine what to charge by factoring in:
- Desired annual take-home pay
- Self-employment taxes (15.3%)
- Health insurance costs
- Business expenses
- Non-billable hours (admin, marketing, invoicing)

## Why This Exists

Most freelancers undercharge by 30-50% because they don't account for:
- The 15.3% self-employment tax (Social Security + Medicare)
- Health insurance ($6,000-$12,000/year)
- Time spent on non-billable work (30-40% of hours)
- Business expenses and software costs
